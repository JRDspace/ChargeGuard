ChargeGuard controls a smart plug based on battery percentage.

Use it at your own risk. It is provided as-is, without warranty. Hardware,
operating system sleep behavior, WiZ plug connectivity, network changes, and
power settings can affect whether charger connect/disconnect actions happen
at the expected time.

ChargeGuard is a helper tool, not a guarantee of battery health, power safety,
or device protection. Keep normal charging precautions in place.

If the software is not running, it cannot manage the smart plug. Keep normal
charging precautions in place.
